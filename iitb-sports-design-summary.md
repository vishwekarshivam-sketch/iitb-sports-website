# IITB Sports Website — Design Summary Reference

## For Human & AI Reference

The IITB Sports website redesign is structured in two distinct phases that contrast intentionally. **Phase 1** is a cinematic dark entry page — full-viewport Gymkhana video background with a 60% black overlay, left-aligned massive display type cycling through "FOR / EVERY / [ATHLETES]*" in the style of Lewis Hamilton's website and Nike's typographic boldness, with social links, legal text top-right, and a single CTA to enter the main site. **Phase 2** is the main website — a warm cream (`#F5F0E8`) editorial light theme that directly contrasts the dark entry, using a Bento grid system for the Sports directory, full-bleed alternating dark and light sections, asymmetric layouts with giant number anchors, and two infinite marquee ticker strips (one gold background carrying sport names, one dark background carrying achievements in the opposite scroll direction). The content sections cover: a split-layout Hero with stats chips, an About Us section with the "Until. Victory. Always." tagline, a full-dark Bombay BlackCats section, and a white Sports App section. The color system uses warm cream as the primary background, near-black `#111111` for dark sections, and gold `#C9A84C` as the sole accent threaded across both phases — appearing on hover states, active nav indicators, marquee backgrounds, card borders, and CTAs. The typography system pairs **Barlow Condensed Black** (display, hero, stats, marquee — uppercase, high impact) with **Playfair Display Bold** (section headings — editorial warmth) and **DM Sans** (body copy — clean and legible), with **DM Mono** used for all labels, eyebrows, and tags. Motion is purposeful: staggered load reveals, scroll-triggered fade-ups, constant marquee animation, and subtle card hover lifts. The navbar is sticky with a transparent-to-white transition on scroll, a gold active underline, and a gold "Book a Court" pill CTA. The overall design language is **premium editorial sports** — confident, modern, and contrasting between phases, with the Bento grid, marquee strips, grain texture on cream sections, and giant typographic anchors as the signature aesthetic moves.
