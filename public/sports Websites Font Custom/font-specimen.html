In a thrilling finale that left the capacity crowd breathless, the IITB football team clinched the Inter-IIT gold medal in the final seconds of extra time. Captain Arjun Mehta's decisive header — his third of the tournament — sealed a 3–2 victory over a formidable IITK side that had dominated possession for much of the second half. The win marks the third consecutive Inter-IIT football gold for IITB, a record unmatched in the competition's forty-year history.
